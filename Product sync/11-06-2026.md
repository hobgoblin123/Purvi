**Key decision — principles that get ignored become gates.** "Consistency is a feature" was promoted to Janmam on 10-06 and violated on 11-06. A principle with no enforcement step is just a comment. So: every *actionable* principle must have a matching checklist gate. Janmam stays as the reasoning/reflection record (the "why"); the checklist is the enforcement (the "do this now"). Applied retrospectively to the un-enforced older principles, not just today's.

**Added 4 checklist gates:**

- *During building* — **Shared-infra blast radius** (new; from the rate-limit outage).
- *After building* — **Verify end-to-end as the real user/role** (enforces "Consistency is a feature"; from the VAPID-as-root bug).
- *After building* — **Test the logged-out path** (enforces "Cookieless logged-out surface").

**Added 2 Janmam principles:** "Verify end-to-end as the real user/role" and "A change's blast radius includes shared infrastructure."
